## Welcome to Roxom Rulebook

Your comprehensive guide to trading on Roxom's Bitcoin-denominated platform.

<Note>
  All trading on Roxom is conducted in Bitcoin. All contracts are denominated and settled in BTC.
</Note>

## About This Rulebook

This rulebook covers all aspects of trading on Roxom, from basic concepts to advanced risk management mechanisms. Each section provides detailed explanations of how our platform operates.
